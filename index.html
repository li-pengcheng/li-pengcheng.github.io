Short Bio

I am now a Ph.D. candidate in the Intelligent Multimedia Processing Research Group at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Chenqiang Gao. Before that, I got my B.Eng and M.Eng degrees from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2017 and 2020, respectively.

My research interest is Medical Image Analysis, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.
