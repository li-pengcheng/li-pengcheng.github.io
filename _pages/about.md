Greeting! My name is Pengcheng Li(李鹏程). I received my Master degree from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2020. I am now  a Ph.D. student (Second year) in School of Communications and Information Engineering at Chongqing University of Posts and telecommunications, China. I am a member of the  intelligent media group (IMG) under the supervision of Chenqiang Gao. My research interests include: Medical Image Analysis, Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
